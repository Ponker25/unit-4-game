Crystal Collector

You will be given a random target number to start the game.

Rules of the Game

1. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount to your number.

2. To win the game, match your total score to the target number, you lose if your score goes above the target number.

3. The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
